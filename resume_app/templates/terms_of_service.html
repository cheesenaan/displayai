--- ## **CheeseCV Cookie Policy** ### **Last Updated: November 15, 2024** Welcome to CheeseCV! This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website and SaaS platform. By using our services, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy. ### **1. What Are Cookies?** Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help us understand how you interact with our site, remember your preferences, and improve your overall experience. ### **2. Why We Use Cookies** We use cookies for various reasons, including to: - **Ensure Proper Functionality**: Some cookies are essential for the functionality of our website, such as managing user sessions and enabling secure login. - **Improve User Experience**: Cookies help us remember your preferences (e.g., theme selection, form data) to enhance your experience. - **Security**: To protect your account from unauthorized access and ensure the security of your data. - **Analytics and Performance**: To analyze how users interact with our site, helping us optimize our services. ### **3. Types of Cookies We Use** #### **a. Essential Cookies** These cookies are necessary for the website to function correctly and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are typically set in response to your actions, such as logging in or filling out forms. - **Examples**: - `sessionid`: Maintains your login session across pages. - `csrftoken`: Protects against Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) attacks. #### **b. Functional Cookies** These cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings, such as account details or theme selections. - **Examples**: - `account_id`: Used to identify your account when accessing personalized features like `/account/`, `/form/`, or `/subscriptions/`. #### **c. Analytical Cookies** These cookies help us understand how our users interact with the site by collecting anonymous data on page visits, clicks, and other user behaviors. This helps us improve our website's functionality and user experience. - **Examples**: - We may use third-party analytics tools like Google Analytics, which set cookies to track user activity. #### **d. Marketing and Advertising Cookies** We may use these cookies to deliver personalized content and advertisements that are relevant to your interests. These are primarily used if you interact with our promotional offers or landing pages. - **Examples**: - `utm_campaign`, `utm_source`: Track the source of your visit if you came through a marketing campaign. ### **4. Third-Party Cookies** Our website may include cookies set by third-party services, such as payment processors (e.g., Stripe) when managing subscriptions, or social media platforms for sharing content. - **Examples**: - Stripe cookies for managing payments like `/payment_successful` and `/payment_cancelled`. - Social media buttons may set cookies for platforms like Facebook, LinkedIn, or Twitter. ### **5. Managing and Deleting Cookies** You have the right to accept or decline cookies. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our site. - **Browser Settings**: - [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647) - [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/enable-and-disable-cookies-website-preferences) - [Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac) - [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09) ### **6. Cookie Duration** The cookies we use can be categorized as either session cookies or persistent cookies: - **Session Cookies**: These cookies are temporary and expire once you close your browser. - **Persistent Cookies**: These cookies remain on your device for a specified period or until manually deleted. ### **7. Changes to This Cookie Policy** We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
