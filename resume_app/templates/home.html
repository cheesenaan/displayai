About us

At our resume web app, we help you create a professional resume in just 5 minutes. All you need to do is input your basic information, and we will provide you with a Google Doc link to your resume.

Resume Score of 95+/100

Companies use an ATS scanner to scan your resume. Our platform uses artificial intelligence and data science models to ensure that your resume score is 95+/100. This means that you can download your resume as a PDF from your Google Doc link and directly start applying to internships.

Designed for Internships

Our artificial intelligence and data science models are designed specifically for internships. This means that you will hear back quickly from potential employers, giving you a better chance of securing your dream internship.
